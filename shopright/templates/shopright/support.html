Getting Started

How do I scan my first receipt?
1. Open GroceryGuard and tap the "Scan" tab
2. Position your camera over a grocery receipt
3. Tap the capture button or let it auto-capture
4. Wait a few seconds while AI extracts all items
5. Review and edit items if needed, then save
What types of receipts work?
GroceryGuard works with receipts from any grocery store, supermarket, or food retailer. The AI is trained to recognize standard receipt formats including store name, items, prices, and dates.
How do I create a shopping list?
Tap the "List" tab to see your shopping list. You can:
• Add items manually by tapping the + button
• Tap "Add from History" to see past purchases
• Select items you want to buy again
• Check off items as you shop

Troubleshooting

Receipt scan isn't working or items are missing
• Ensure good lighting and the receipt is flat and clear
• Make sure the entire receipt is visible in the frame
• Avoid shadows or glare on the receipt
• After scanning, you can manually add any missed items using the Edit button
• Try taking a photo of the receipt instead of scanning it directly
I can't sign in with my phone number
• Make sure you have a stable internet connection
• Verify the phone number is correct (include country code)
• Check that you can receive SMS messages
• If you don't receive the code within 60 seconds, tap "Resend"
• Try restarting the app if the issue persists
My purchase history isn't syncing
• Pull down on the History tab to refresh
• Check your internet connection
• Make sure you're signed in to the same account on all devices
• Data syncs automatically when connected to Wi-Fi or cellular
Barcode scanner isn't finding nutrition info
Not all products are in our nutrition database yet. We're constantly expanding coverage. If a product isn't found, you can still see the basic item information from your receipt.

How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Open iPhone Settings
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select GroceryGuard
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

You'll retain Premium access until the end of your billing period.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
1. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase Premium
2. Open GroceryGuard
3. Go to Settings/Account
4. Tap "Restore Purchases"

Your Premium status will be restored automatically.
I was charged but don't have Premium access
Try tapping "Restore Purchases" in Settings. If that doesn't work, contact us with your purchase receipt and we'll resolve it immediately.

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure?
Yes. GroceryGuard uses industry-standard encryption and secure Firebase authentication. Your data is stored securely in the cloud and only accessible by you. We never sell your personal information.
Can I delete my account and data?
Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data including receipts, shopping lists, and purchase history. This action cannot be undone.
