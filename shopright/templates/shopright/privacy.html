ShopRight Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

In Brief: ShopRight collects receipt images, barcode scans, and shopping data to provide personalized grocery assistance. We do not sell your data. Your information is encrypted and secure.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Phone number (for authentication via Firebase)
  • Profile information (if provided)

1.2 Receipt Data

  • Receipt images (stored securely)
  • Store name, location, and purchase date
  • Product names, quantities, and prices
  • Total spending amounts

1.3 Barcode Scanning

  • UPC/EAN barcodes scanned
  • Product photos (if uploaded)
  • Nutrition lookup history

1.4 Shopping Lists

  • List names and items
  • Check-off status and notes
  • Shared list membership

1.5 Family Sharing

  • Family group membership
  • Invite codes and join history
  • Shared shopping data within family

1.6 Recall Alerts (Premium)

  • Recall match history
  • User responses (confirm/dismiss)
  • Notification preferences

1.7 Usage Analytics

  • App usage patterns and features used
  • Device type and OS version
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Provide receipt scanning and storage Receipt images, purchase data
Nutrition information lookup Barcodes, product photos
Food recall monitoring Purchase history, product names, UPCs
Shopping list management List items, preferences
Family sharing Family membership, shared data
Spending analytics Purchase totals, store visits
Improve the app Usage analytics, crash reports
Customer support Account info, reported issues

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

ShopRight does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.2 Third-Party Services

We share limited data with:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and user management
  • OpenAI: Receipt parsing and text extraction (anonymized)
  • Open Food Facts: Nutrition database queries (barcodes only)
  • Apple App Store: Subscription management and payments

3.3 Family Sharing

When you join a family group, your shopping history and lists are visible to all family members.

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Authentication: Firebase phone authentication with secure tokens
  • Access Control: Strict server-side authorization checks
  • Secure Storage: Receipt images stored on secure cloud servers

5. Data Retention

  • Free Users: 30-day receipt history
  • Premium Users: Unlimited receipt history
  • Deleted Accounts: Data purged within 30 days
  • Shopping Lists: Retained until manually deleted
  • Recall Alerts: Retained for 1 year or until dismissed

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Export

You can view all your data in the app. Contact us for a data export.

6.2 Correction

Edit receipt data, shopping lists, and profile information in the app.

6.3 Deletion

Request account deletion by contacting support. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

6.4 California Residents (CCPA)

California users have additional rights to know, delete, and opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data).

7. Children's Privacy

ShopRight is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Users

ShopRight is operated in the United States. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your data to the U.S.

9. Cookies and Tracking

  • We use Firebase for analytics (device ID, usage patterns)
  • No third-party advertising cookies
  • You can disable analytics in device settings

10. Push Notifications

We send notifications for:

  • Food recall alerts (if premium)
  • Shopping list reminders (if enabled)
  • You can disable notifications in device settings

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to external websites (e.g., recall sources). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.
