Frequently Asked Questions

How do I link a bank account?

Go to the Accounts tab and tap "Link Account." You'll be guided through Plaid's secure bank connection process. Your bank credentials are never shared with us.

How do I invite my partner?

Go to Settings and tap "Invite Someone." Share the invite code with your partner via text, email, or any messaging app.

How do low balance alerts work?

Set a threshold in Settings under "Low Balance Alert." When any account drops below that amount, both you and your partner will receive a push notification.

Is my bank data secure?

Yes. Shillak uses Plaid, the same service used by Venmo, Cash App, and thousands of other financial apps. Your bank credentials are handled entirely by Plaid — we never see or store them.

How do I remove a bank account?

Go to the Accounts tab, swipe left on the account you want to remove, and tap "Remove."
