Questions?

Is my financial data safe?

Yes. Shillak uses Plaid — the same secure backend trusted by Venmo, Robinhood, and Coinbase — to connect to your bank. Your credentials never touch our servers. We don't sell data, we don't run ads, and everything is encrypted in transit.

Which banks does Shillak support?

Over 12,000 financial institutions including Bank of America, Chase, Capital One, Wells Fargo, US Bank, PNC, Citi, Discover, Ally, and more.

How do I share with my partner?

Open Settings → Invite Someone → tap a contact. They get a link. They tap. Shillak opens (or installs from the App Store) and joins your Home instantly. No codes to type.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Manage your subscription in Settings → Subscription → Manage Subscription, or directly through your Apple ID account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Does Shillak work on Android?

Not yet. Shillak is iPhone-only for now. Android support is on the roadmap.
